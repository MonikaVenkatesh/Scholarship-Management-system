OUR MISSION

The Mission of IGNITE is to provide scholarships to exceptionaly bright students who have financial constraints,enabling them to achieve professional success as Engineers and Doctors.


We fund talented students and help them to pursue sought-after courses in India .Their exceptional academic performance has helped them gain admission to the most reputed Engineering and Medical colleges in India.They have proved to be role models in the community,inspiring others to pursue this path.
